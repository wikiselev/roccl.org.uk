Welcome to our website!


We are an Orthodox Parish in Luton, UK of the Diocese of Sourozh - the diocese of the Russian Orthodox Church which covers the British Isles. Our services are held in general, twice each month: the Typikha on the first Sunday and the Divine Liturgy on the third Sunday. Both services begin at 10:30am.

The Parish

Our bishop is Bishop Matthew of Sourozh and the community is served by Fr. Anthony Ilin. For the most part our people are of British and Russian background and our services are celebrated in English, the lingua franca of the community, with some Church Slavonic. You are most welcome to visit us or to make your spiritual home with us.

Join Us

If you are just a visitor, you are welcome to explore the website and to learn more about the Parish. If you are our parishioner and you would like to receive regular newsletters and updates from us, please like our Facebook page or contact our church warden by email.
